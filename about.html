About the Artist

Catherine Cummings

"Curiosity is my drive! I will never hesitate to open the box."

My name is Catherine Cummings. I am a multi facited artist with an innate drive to create. I have been interacting with the creative world for as long as I can recall, and have always been eager to explore new skills. My curiosity has given me the motivation to dabble in many mediums, such as sketching, colored pencils, digital works, crochet, knitting, and so much more! Even this website was entirely created by me from the ground up. Going forward, I hope to explore as much of the creative world as possible, and capture that joy in my creations.
